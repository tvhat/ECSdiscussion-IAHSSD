Schedule of a session

The schedule describes an one-hour session with 20-40 people, which could be extended to accommodate for more interaction. Depending on the session, we deviated slightly from the schedule responding to the course of the discussion.

1. Introduction (5 min) 
	History of the decades 
	Aim of the initiative 
	Planning of the session 
	Questions 

2. Break-out groups (25 min) 
	Discussing the questions 
	4-5 people 
	As diverse as possible (institute, sub-discipline) 

3. Plenary explanation (10 min) 
	Summarizing outcome of break-out groups 
	1-2 min per group 

4. Plenary discussion (20 min) 
	Reacting to other groups 
	Answering the prepared questions
